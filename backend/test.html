Bowtie Diagram

A Bowtie diagram as its name suggests has the form of a bowtie. The central object in the diagram describes an unwanted event. On its left side are situated the potential causes of this event and on its right side are situated the consequences from it. The most significant advantage of a Bowtie diagram compared to other tools for risk analyses is that it represents in a comprehensive and intuitive way the relation between causes and consequences in different scenarios.
