How to fill-in the Impact matrix ?


To compute the impact of a consequence, link it to an impact matrix that associates a level of severity to each criterion below. To fill the matrix, select a dot on each line from "not severe"(green), to "extremely severe"(red). To save your modifications on this page, don't forget to click on the "Subtim" button at the bottom.




Commercial : severity of the consequence on the commercial activity of the organisation that owns the system

Reputation : severity of the consequence on the reputation of the organisation that owns the system


Environment : severity of the consequence on the environment

Individual : severity of the consequence on the individuals around the system
