How to fill the Impact matrix ?




To compute the impact of a consequence, link it to an impact matrix that associates a level of severity to each criteria below. To fill the matrix, select a dot on each line from "not severe"(green) to "extremely severe"(red).





Individual : severity of the consequence on the individuals around the system

Environment : severity of the consequence on the environment

Reputation : severity of the consequence on the reputation of the organisation that possesses the system

Commercial : severity of the consequence on the commercial activity of the organisation that possesses the system
