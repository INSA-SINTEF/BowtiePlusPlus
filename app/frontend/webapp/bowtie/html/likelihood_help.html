How to fill-in the Likelihood matrix ?


To compute the likelihood of a threat, link it to a likelihood matrix that associates a level of danger to each criterion below. To fill the matrix, select a dot on each line from "not dangerous"(green) to "extremely dangerous"(red). To save your modifications on this page, don't forget to click on the "Submit" button at the bottom.




Actors : the level of skills needed to perform the attack


Opportunity : the presence of a favorable combination of circumstances that makes an action possible


Means : resources needed to perform the attack


Motivation : reasons that lead the attacker to perform the attack
